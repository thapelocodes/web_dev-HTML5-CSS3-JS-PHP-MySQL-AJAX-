KOTA Mikey - BYNM Album Art

The BYNM Album Art is the signature cover of KOTA Mikey's debut tape Before You Notice Me released on April 14th 2023. The body of work was exclusively produced by lostboy, seazn and Mikey, as well as executively produced by IJ Talam. It has no features and tells the story of a young, up and coming artist with a dream to chase.
